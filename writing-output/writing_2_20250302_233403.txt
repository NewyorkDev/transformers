Topic: how to clean shower head:

how to clean shower head: 1. Fill a plastic bag with white vinegar or baking soda and water solution (50/50). 2. Secure the bag over the showerhead using a rubber band, ensuring that all parts are submerged in the solution. 3. Let it soak for 30 minutes (for white vineger) or overnight (for baking sodea). 4. Remove the bag and rinse off the shower head under running warm water. 5. If mineral deposits persist, use an old toothbrush dipped in the same solution to scrub them away gently. 6. Repeat as necessary until your showerhead is completely cleaned. 7. To prevent future buildup, consider wrapping the shower hose with a cloth dampened with white distilled vinegar every few months.
